What Makes The “XO Foodie Tour” the Premiere Vietnam Food Tour?

Unlike most other food tours in Vietnam, we steer clear of common dishes like Pho, Banh Xeo, Banh Mi and Spring Rolls. Why book a tour that serves you food you can get on every Vietnamese street corner?

At XO Tours, we aim to provide a truly unique food experience with an itinerary thats difficult for most travelers to replicate. That’s why the “XO Foodie” is the most acclaimed Vietnam food tour, and is designed to introduce you to lesser-known dishes that most blogs and tours overlook!

We are also THE ONLY Vietnam food tour to recognized by Forbes as one of the top 9 food tours in the world, named one of the top 10 city tours in the world by USA Today, and praised by Food & Wine as the best food tour in Vietnam. The New York Times also lists us as one of the top things to do in Ho Chi Minh City

Most other food tours in Saigon visit the same locations, like the “Ho Thi Ky Flower Market” and the “Nguyen Thien Thuat” apartments, so the tourists often outnumber the locals! Why are these places so popular with other tour operators? Because they offer cheap, low quality street food that are carb-heavy to fill you up. Both these locations are also super easy to visit on your own and don’t really require a tour guide.

While there are many other cheaper food tours in Vietnam, it is IMPOSSIBLE to provide the superior experience that XO Tours offers for a much lower price without cutting corners. The majority of the copycat tours operate illegally, serve super low quality food, and hire poorly trained students, which seriously affects the quality and safety of your experience. Guests that have gone on other Vietnam food tours and The XO Foodie Tour always tell us that the difference is like NIGHT and DAY!

A Vietnam Food Tour for the Adventurous!

On the XO Foodie Tour we will take you to restaurants and food stalls hidden far outside the tourist areas and show you where the locals really love to eat!

Our menu is diverse and offers a great selection of meats, seafood, fresh vegetables and fruits and include very little carbs or gluten. We also won’t make you eat dog meat, bugs, organs, raw food or anything too weird.

UNLIMITED food drinks are included in the price and we can easily adjust the menu for vegans/vegetarians and most other food restrictions! We can also offer child friendly dishes for families with young children!

So save your appetite, delight your palate and let us fill your tummy with delicious and authentic Vietnamese food!

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you accommodate Vegans and Vegetarians on your tours?

Absolutely! We take vegans and vegetarians on our tours almost daily, and prior guests love the variety and quality of the dishes we provide!

Our vegan and vegetarian guests will be provided just as much food or more than other guests on the tour.

For a glimpse as the type of dishes we offer to vegan/vegetarian guests on the XO Foodie Tour click on the video link below:

https://www.instagram.com/reel/C-9pEijPwWR/?igsh=bWUxZzhjN214Z2ty

Please keep in mind that our tours are by default group tours so non-vegan/vegetarian guests will be served dishes containing meat.

Can you cater to my food allergies and other restrictions?

If given prior notice, we can adjust the dishes on our tours to accommodate almost any food restriction. Dont like seafood no problem. Vegetarians/Vegans weve also got you covered. We can also deal with gluten allergies, nut allergies, shellfish allergies, etc.

Please let us know about your food restrictions, at the time you book your tour, so that we can prepare alternative options.

If you are allergic to fish however, we do not recommend booking our food tour as most dishes in Vietnam are marinated with generous amounts of fish sauce. If youre allergic to fish sauce then the only safe dishes for you to eat are the vegetarian dishes.

We also dont recommend booking a tour if you are allergic to MSG, as most Vietnamese dishes will contain MSG (including vegetarian dishes).
